Privacy Policy for Bracket Buddy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

Overview

Bracket Buddy is a mountain bike suspension tuning tool that uses AI coaching to help riders dial in their setup. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

Data We Collect

  • Conversation data — messages you send to the AI coach are processed to generate responses
  • Suspension settings — any bracket data or settings you enter into the app
  • Device information — basic technical info like device type and iOS version for debugging

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide AI coaching responses through our secure backend server
  • To save your conversation history locally on your device
  • To improve the app experience

What We Don't Do

  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising
  • We do not store your conversations on our servers after your session ends

Third Party Services

Bracket Buddy uses Anthropic's Claude AI to power the coaching feature. Messages you send are processed through Anthropic's API. You can review Anthropic's privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy.

Data Storage

Conversation history is stored locally on your device only. You can clear your history at any time by deleting the app.

Children's Privacy

Bracket Buddy is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
